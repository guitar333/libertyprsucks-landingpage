The Truth Behind the Service

At Liberty PR Sucks, we’re dedicated to documenting the numerous issues with Liberty Puerto Rico’s mobile and internet service. Since the acquisition of AT&T’s operations, many customers have faced poor service, dropped calls, billing problems, and long wait times for customer support. Despite efforts to improve, Liberty continues to lose the trust of its customers.

Switch to Starlink for Reliable Internet

Starlink, powered by SpaceX, offers a cutting-edge satellite internet service that doesn’t rely on Liberty's infrastructure. This means you can avoid the outages and service issues caused by Liberty’s legacy systems. Starlink provides high-speed, low-latency internet, even in rural and underserved areas of Puerto Rico.

  • Pricing: Starlink’s hardware is now priced at $299 (down from $499), and the monthly service fee is $99.
  • Availability: Starlink has expanded its reach in Puerto Rico, with the latest generation of hardware available at Home Depot and other retailers.
  • Why it’s better: Starlink operates independently of local infrastructure, meaning you won’t be impacted by Liberty’s frequent outages.
